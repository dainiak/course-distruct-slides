Field \( \bbF \) with two operations \( + \) and \( \cdot \).
  • commutative and associative laws,
  • distributive law of "multiplication" over "addition",
  • in \( \bbF \) there exist such \( 0 \) and \( 1 \) that \( 0+a=a \) for any \( a\in\bbF \) and \( 1\cdot b=b \) for any \( b\in\bbF\setminus\{0\} \).
  • an equation of the form \( a+x=b \) for any \( a, b\in\bbF \) has a solution,
  • an equation of the form \( a\cdot x=b \) for any \( a\in\bbF\setminus\{0\} \) and \( b\in\bbF \) has a solution.
